About Me

Hello, I'm Haonan Cheng, a passionate photographer with a keen eye for capturing life's meaningful moments. With over 10 years of experience behind the lens, I specialize in portrait, landscape, and documentary photography.

My work aims to reveal the extraordinary in the ordinary, finding beauty in unexpected places and moments. Each photograph tells a story - whether it's the subtle interplay of light and shadow or the raw emotion on a subject's face.

Based in Shanghai, I travel extensively for projects and am always open to new creative collaborations. Feel free to browse my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to work together.
